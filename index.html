About Us

Crafting excellence since 1985

Our Story

For over three decades, The Classic Cut has been the premier destination for gentlemen seeking traditional barbering services with a modern touch. Our commitment to excellence and attention to detail has made us a cornerstone of the community.

Founded by master barber James Sullivan in 1985, our shop has maintained the time-honored traditions of classic barbering while embracing contemporary styles and techniques. Each member of our team brings years of experience and a passion for their craft.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We deliver the highest quality grooming services
  • Tradition: We honor classic barbering techniques
  • Innovation: We embrace modern styles and trends
  • Community: We build lasting relationships with our clients
